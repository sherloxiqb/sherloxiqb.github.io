Petroleum Engineer

  • IATMI22-017-SPC - Techno-Economic Evaluation for CCUS-EOR Implementation on Oil Reservoir in Indonesia: A Case Study in “B” Structure at “S” Field

    Published at Simposium IATMI 2022. Acted as First Author. This study evaluated the CO2 enhanced oil recovery performance by conducted a case study in Indonesia; modelled the cost of complete chain of capture, transport, and storage of CCUS, used reservoir simulation in selected oil field to forecast the performance of EOR, and tabulated using cost recovery and gross split; uncovered 4% higher oil recovery with profit up to 6.26 USD/tCO2.

  • IPA22-SE-194 - Comparative Study on Fixed-Price Contract versus Incentive-Based Contract in Indonesia's Oil and Gas Industry

    Published at The 46th IPA Convention and Exhibiton 2022. Acted as Second Author. This study proposed new strategic alliance and partnership model to SKK Migas called incentive-based contract; conducted Monte Carlo simulation to the production data into 1000 cases with several baseline parameters and illustrated using analytical equations; resolved 5+ challenges in the common fixed-price contract by using the proposed model
